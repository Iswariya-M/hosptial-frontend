Welcome to Admin Dashboard

This is the admin dashboard section. You can manage doctors and appointments here. The admin dashboard provides a comprehensive overview and management capabilities for the hospital's operations.

Manage Doctors

In this section, you can add new doctors, update existing doctor information, and delete doctors who are no longer part of the hospital. Each doctor profile includes details such as name, speciality, date of birth, age, location, and phone number. Keeping this information up-to-date ensures that patients can easily find and book appointments with the right specialist for their needs.

Manage Appointments

The appointment management section allows you to view all upcoming appointments, sorted by date. You can see details of each appointment, including the patient's name, contact information, the doctor they are seeing, and the appointment date. You also have the ability to add new appointments and cancel existing ones. Efficient appointment management helps in reducing wait times and improving patient satisfaction.

Doctor Specialities

Understanding the various specialities available at the hospital is crucial for both admin staff and patients. The specialities range from general medicine, cardiology, dermatology, orthopaedics, and more. Each speciality comes with a set of expertise that addresses specific health concerns. By categorizing doctors based on their specialities, the admin can ensure that patients receive the best possible care tailored to their medical needs.

The admin dashboard is a vital tool in the efficient management of the hospital. By leveraging its features, you can ensure that the hospital runs smoothly, providing high-quality care to all patients. Stay organized, keep information current, and utilize the analytics to drive improvements in all aspects of hospital administration.
