Dermatology

Dermatology

Overview

Overview

The excellence in dermatological care at SRM Global Hospitals Pvt Ltd. Our Dermatology Department is dedicated to treating a spectrum of skin, hair, and nail diseases. Beyond medical management, we specialize in aesthetic and cosmetic dermatology, providing treatments like injectables, lasers, facials, and peels to enhance your skin and hair’s appearance.

Why Choose Us?

Expertise and Experience:

At SRM Global Hospitals Pvt Ltd, we take pride in our team of board-certified plastic surgeons, dermatologists, and skilled healthcare professionals who bring years of experience and expertise to the table. Our specialists stay abreast of the latest advancements in aesthetic and cosmetic procedures, ensuring you receive the highest standard of care.

Personalized Approach:

We understand that each individual is unique, and so are their aesthetic goals. Our commitment to a personalized approach means that we tailor our treatments to your specific needs and desires. Through thorough consultations, we work closely with you to create a customized treatment plan that aligns with your aesthetic vision.

State-of-the-Art Facilities:

Step into a world of modern and advanced facilities at SRM Global Hospitals Pvt Ltd. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure is equipped with the latest technologies, providing you with a safe and comfortable environment for your aesthetic journey. From consultation rooms to procedure suites, every aspect of our facility is designed to meet the highest standards.

Comprehensive Range of Services:

Our Aesthetic, Plastic, and Cosmetic Department offer a comprehensive range of services, encompassing both surgical and non-surgical options. Whether you are considering facial rejuvenation, body contouring, or skin enhancement, our diverse portfolio of treatments caters to a wide spectrum of aesthetic goals.

Patient-Centric Care:

Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities. We believe in fostering a patient-centric environment where open communication and trust thrive. Our caring and compassionate staff are here to guide you through every step of your aesthetic journey, ensuring you feel informed, supported, and confident in your decisions.

Commitment to Safety:

Your safety is paramount to us. We adhere to the highest standards of safety protocols and hygiene practices, providing you with peace of mind throughout your aesthetic experience. Our commitment to safety extends to the use of quality products and the implementation of rigorous procedures to minimize any potential risks.

Why Choose Us?

Comprehensive Medical Management Services

Employing the latest medical evidence and techniques, our dermatologists ensure the highest quality care for skin, hair, and nail diseases.

Wide Range of Treatments

From common skin infections and acne to more complex conditions like psoriasis, vitiligo, and skin cancers, we offer a comprehensive array of medical management services.

Specialized Therapeutic Services

We ensure accurate diagnostics and effective treatment by utilizing advanced techniques including diagnostic skin biopsies, direct immunofluorescence staining, immunotherapy, and more.

Cosmetic Services for Aesthetic Enhancement

Our cosmetic services include chemical peeling, Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy, microneedling, electrosurgery, radiofrequency ablation, and LASER treatments for hair reduction, pigmentation, and tattoo removal.
